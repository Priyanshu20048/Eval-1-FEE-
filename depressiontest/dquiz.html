Depression Test

1. Have you been feeling down, depressed, or hopeless?

2. Have you had little interest or pleasure in doing things?

3. Have you experienced trouble falling or staying asleep, or sleeping too much?

4. Have you been feeling tired or having little energy?

5. Have you had a poor appetite or been overeating?

6. Have you been feeling bad about yourself — or that you are a failure or have let yourself or your family down?

7. Have you had trouble concentrating on things, such as reading the newspaper or watching television?

8. Have you been moving or speaking so slowly that other people could have noticed? Or the opposite — being so fidgety or restless that you have been moving around a lot more than usual?

9. Have you had thoughts that you would be better off dead, or thoughts of hurting yourself in some way?

10. Have you found it difficult to cope with the stress and demands of daily life?
